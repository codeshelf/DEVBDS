- Material: FR4
- Thickness: 0.062"
- Copper weight: 1 oz
- Finish: ENIG
- All vias tented (included in soldermask layers)
- Solder mask: BLACK
- Silk screen: WHITE (top only)
- Hole diameters applicable after plating
- UL796 Flammability Rating 94V-0. Fabricator's UL Logo shall be placed on bottom side of board
- Date of manufacture and lot code shall be placed on bottom side of board
- Stackup: 
  Top Paste         - BRAIN_Breakout_SW.SPT
  Top Silkscreen    - BRAIN_Breakout_SW.SST 
  Top Soldermask    - BRAIN_Breakout_SW.SMT
  Top Copper        - BRAIN_Breakout_SW.LAY1
  Bottom Copper     - BRAIN_Breakout_SW.LAY4
  Bottom Soldermask - BRAIN_Breakout_SW.SMB
  Bottom Silkscreen - BRAIN_Breakout_SW.SSB
  Bottom Paste      - BRAIN_Breakout_SW.SPB
  Excellon Drill    - BRAIN_Breakout_SW.drd
  Board Outline     - BRAIN_Breakout_SW.FAB - note internal cutout
  Component RefDes  - BRAIN_Breakout_SW.REF - component names not in sikscreen (for assembly use) 
  Component centroid- BRAIN_Breakout_SW_centroid.csv
